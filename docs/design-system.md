# Design system

## Direction

Calm academic command centre: editorial whitespace, cream canvas, true-white product surfaces, deep navy copy, blue actions, red signals and small gold details from the supplied lion/dragon/compass mark.

## Tokens

- Canvas `#f7f5ef`
- Surface `#ffffff`
- Ink `#071a3a`
- Muted ink `#526078`
- Sync blue `#1460d2`
- Signal red `#e5243d`
- Support teal `#087b72`
- Gold `#c89122`
- Line `#dcded8`

Geist is self-hosted by `next/font`. Geist Mono is reserved for small sequence and status labels. The content container is 1240 px with fluid gutters.

## Motifs

- Red and blue paths show fragmented information converging into an organised view.
- Product scenes are code-native, contain fictional data and use one consistent component family.
- White interface surfaces use restrained borders and shadows; no glassmorphism or fake 3D.
- Motion is limited to short hover/focus feedback and is removed under `prefers-reduced-motion`.
