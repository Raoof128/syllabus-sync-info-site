# Content gap report

The following items were intentionally not invented and remain production decisions:

1. Final support, security, partnership, media and accessibility email addresses.
2. Approved contact-delivery provider, retention period and privacy processor wording.
3. Complete main-application data inventory, retention, deletion and third-party processor details.
4. Verified current pricing or free-plan statement.
5. Verified supported-institution list.
6. Verified feature-by-feature availability, beta and pilot status from the main application.
7. Authentication, authorisation, session, encryption and account-deletion evidence for the main application.
8. Institution partnerships, endorsements, testimonials, ratings, user counts and certifications; none are rendered.
9. Team names, location and approved programme/incubator wording.
10. External status provider and incident process.
11. Final legal approval for terms and privacy.
12. Brand-asset ownership and public-use approval.

The site uses the supplied positioning and product direction while clearly separating it from unverified availability and technical controls.
